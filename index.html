CONCLUSION

Both methods have distinct strengths. Random Forest excels in accuracy, reproducibility, and offline deployment — ideal for production NILM systems. Groq AI offers zero-shot flexibility and natural language reasoning — ideal for exploration and unknown appliances. The choice depends on the use case: precision vs flexibility.
