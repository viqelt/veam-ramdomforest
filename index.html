Conclusion
Both methods have distinct strengths. Random Forest excels in accuracy, reproducibility, and offline deployment — ideal for production NILM. Groq AI offers zero-shot flexibility and natural language reasoning — ideal for exploration and unknown appliances. The optimal architecture combines both: RF for precision, LLM for adaptability.
