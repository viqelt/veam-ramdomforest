How it works:
K-Means was trained on the DSUAL dataset (Almeria University, Spain) — 6 OZM smart meters recording voltage, current, power, power factor and frequency every ~1 second.

Cluster centers (power signatures) learned during training are compared to your live Google Sheets data. Each reading is assigned to the nearest cluster = appliance detection.

Features: active_power · current · pf · apparent_power · reactive_power · power_range
